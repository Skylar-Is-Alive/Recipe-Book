# Pulled Pork
## Ingedients
### Meat and Potatoes
- Pork (Shoulder)
- Onions
- Buns

### Spices
- [Enchilada Sauce](url)
- Salt
- Chipotle
- Chili Powder
- Cumin
- Paprika
- Coriander

### Garnish
- Carrots
- Onions

### Standards
- Neutral Oil

## Method
### Sous Chef 1
1. Make enchilada sauce if needed
2. Dice onions
3. Put both into crock pot

### Head Chef
1. Rub spices into pork
2. Sear pork
3. Put into crock pot and leave on low with taco sauce for 8 hours
4. Tear meat apart after 8 hours

### Sous Chef 2
1. Julienne carrots
2. Julienne onions
3. Put into seperate containers for sandwich assembly

## Eat
1. Put meat, julienned carrots, and onions on bun
2. Consume
