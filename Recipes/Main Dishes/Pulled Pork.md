# Pulled Pork
## Ingedients
### Substance
- Pork (Shoulder)
- Onions

### Spices
- [Enchilada Sauce](url)
- Salt
- Chipotle
- Chili Powder
- Cumin
- Paprika
- Coriander

### Garnish
- Carrots
- Onions

### Standards
- Neutral Oil

## Method
### Sous Chef
1. Make enchilada sauce if needed
2. Dice onions
3. Put both into crock pot
---
1. Julienne carrots
2. Julienne Onions
3. Put into containers for sandwich assembly

### Head Chef
1. Rub spices into pork
2. Sear pork
3. Put into crock pot
4. Leave on low with taco sauce for 8 hours
5. Tear meat apart

## Eat
1. Put meat, julienned carrots, and onions on bun
2. Consume
