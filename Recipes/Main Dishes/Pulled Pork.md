# Pulled Pork
## Ingedients
### Meat and Potatoes
- Pork (Shoulder)
- Onions
- Buns

### Sauces
- [Enchilada Sauce](url)
- Salsa Verde
- Gochujang

### Spices
- Salt
- Chipotle
- Chili Powder
- Cumin
- Paprika
- Coriander

### Garnish
- Carrots
- Onions

### Standards
- Neutral Oil

## Method
### Sous Chef 1
1. Add following ingredients to crock pot:
    1. Enchilada sauce (make if needed)
    2. Salsa verde and gochujang
    3. Onions - diced

### Head Chef
1. Rub spices into pork
2. Sear pork
3. Put into crock pot and leave on low with sauce for approximately 8 hours or until meat is tearable
4. Tear meat apart

### Sous Chef 2
1. Julienne carrots
2. Julienne onions
3. Put into seperate containers for sandwich assembly

## Eat
1. Put meat, julienned carrots, and onions on bun
2. Consume
